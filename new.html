Products

Our 'insight' range of solutions provides different avenues into viewing, collaborating, maintaining and sharing data and information.


insight:api

Use insight:api as either your first step into the insight range or as a means just to get your data web accessible. This services enables you to publish secured or open APIs as either a JSON or GeoJSON RESTful Service.

insight: view

A route into seeing your data and its associated linked data. Underpinned by a range of APIs of your data, insight:view is a searchable web application that provides an insight in a single record. It is tailored around your data and data requirements, can be linked to open-data sources and through ETL services extract data from a wide range of databases.

insight: map

View data on any connected device, anywhere on an interactive map. Underpinned by the range of APIs of your data and using Open-Source JavaScript web mapping libraries, insight: map allows you to view your data and/or Open-Data easily and effortlessly.

insight: notify

A route into seeing your data and its associated linked data. Underpinned by a range of APIs of your data, insight:view is a searchable web application that provides an insight in a single record. It is tailored around your data and data requirements, can be linked to open-data sources and through ETL services extract data from a wide range of databases.

Fully Managed Services

Geo-solutions

We love QGIS and PostGIS. We've extensive experience in these two pieces of open-source software and can provide a range of services to help you on your way to using them. Further more, because we've designed our products and soultions using QGIS and PostGIS, your APIs will fit nicely into them as well. Let's talk to see how we can help you out.

ETL

Extract, Transform and Load services are at the core to all big-data users and instrictions, for moving data between servers, platforms and file types. While you may not consider your data big data, we neverless consider your data important and as such we can use ETL tools to move, join, transform and present your data from or to virtaully any source or location.
