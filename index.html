About us


We are a digital technology company founded in early 2017, providing services and products to enable our clients to 'do more' with their data. d2s offers a range of web enabled applications underpinned by APIs of your data, that can be run on-premises or in the cloud. The three founding members of d2s have between them over 35 years local government experience, predominately in IT services but with a wealth of knowledge and experience in all service areas of local authorities.


d2s also provides consultancy service to a range of customers, especially those in local government, around digital transformation, cloud integration and migration as well as enterprise geographic information systems based on Open-Source. Using Open-Source technologies is at the heart of d2s which enables us to provide our clients value for money and to rapidly develop applications and services.

At the core


Application Program Interfaces: (APIs) are central to d2s products and services.

The growth of cloud services and infrastructure has changed the IT landscape, as such the power, flexibility and ease of accessibility of APIs is becoming more apparent. d2s believes in API-led connectivity to make data accessible to wider audiences through new, agile, better connected applications. What API-led connectivity means for legacy systems and solutions is that the wealth of data, information and knowledge stored within it can be disseminated beyond the confines of a single application.

Solutions

Our 'insight' range of solutions provides different avenues into viewing, collaborating, maintaining and sharing data and information.


insight: api

This first step into the range of insight products. This services enables you to publish secured or open APIs and can be purchased as a standalone service or comes as standard as part of an insight product. Once setup, clients are free to do want they want with their API.

insight: view

A route into seeing your data and its associated linked data. Underpinned by a range of APIs of your data, insight:view is a searchable web application that provides an insight in a single record. It is tailored around your data and data requirements, can be linked to open-data sources and through ETL services extract data from a wide range of databases.

insight: map

View data on any connected device, anywhere on an interactive map. Underpinned by the range of APIs of your data and using Open-Source JavaScript web mapping libraries, insight: map allows you to view your data and/or Open-Data easily and effortlessly.
